Paperless Billing | "Push eBilling"

If you would like to save postage and printing costs inquire about email based billing. Most traditional email billing is done using the "Pull eBilling" approach. This requires the customer to log-in to view their bill. Then multiple steps are needed for the recipient to activate the paper turn-off selection. This method results in a low paper turn-off percentage. Our solution uses "Push eBilling" delivering the bill securely, and eliminating the need to visit the website.
